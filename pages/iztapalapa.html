Objective:

Project of Colectivo Tlacuache Urbano of one of the oldest traditions in Mexico City, in order to inform and promote Mexican traditions, as well as the creation of the website, photo book, documentary and printed book of the carnival with the support of the troupes involved within it.


Users:

Inhabitants of Mexico City and residents of the Iztapalapa mayor's office.


Contributions:

UX: Define proto persons research, user journey, empathy maps, survey creation, survey analysis, sketch, wireframes.

UI: Color palette selection, website prototyping.

Web Design: Structuring pages, adding images and research texts of the collective in the site built with Word Press.
