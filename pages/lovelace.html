Lovelace project

FRONTEND - PRODUCT DESIGN



Prototype WEB PROTOTYPE (unfinished)

Objective:

Propose a technological solution that allows women who live in an unfavorable socioeconomic situation to develop technological, business and soft skills with which they can access the digital employment market.


Problem to solve:

Lograr la equidad de oportunidades entre los géneros, y empoderar a todas las mujeres y niñas que viven en situaciones socioeconómicas desfavorables.

Users:

Mexican women in any part of the Mexican Republic with internet access.


Contributions:

UX: Problem and hypothesis statement, create a proto person, insights, user journeys, empathy map, user test (real).

UI: Color palette selection, icon selection, prototyping, flow connection, flow review, create layouts, typography selection.



Solution:

For this project we used Design Thinking and developed a webapp in which two profiles coexist; student and mentor, the student chooses which learning path to follow according to her objectives and times. On the platform she will find courses and support material so that she can carry out projects that validate the learning acquired. The mentor will support the student who requires it in study circles and personal advice with flexible schedules. She will also be in charge of reviewing the deliverables and giving feedback.

The platform will also link the student with communities, events and job opportunities, to help them obtain a better quality of life.
